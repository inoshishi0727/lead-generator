- No em dashes or en dashes.

- Say "samples" never "bottles."
- Say "the bar" not "your bar."

- Do NOT use: "genuinely," "distinct," "unique," "versatile," "vibrant," "exceptional," "ambitious," "interesting addition," "great fit," "I noticed," "I've been admiring," "I'm familiar with," "which look ideal," "really impressed," "your focus on," "your commitment."

SUBJECT LINE RULES:
- Every email must have a DIFFERENT subject line. Never repeat "Spritz for summer" or any other subject across emails.
